How do I draw my Webtoon?

If you're wondering how I achieve the looks for my comics, then here are the steps I take to make Wild boy my own!

Steps:

Step 1: Sketch. Every art piece starts out with a rough idea! It doesn't need to be perfect! I use the Chalk brush in the Calligraphy pen for this step.

Step 2: Line. I go in with the Syrup pen in the Inking section in Procreate, and clean up my sketch to make it nicer!

Step 3: Color. I use the Color Drop tool to fill in the flat colors of all of the lines!

Step 4: Blur. I Duplicate my lineart layer, then use the Gaussian Blur tool and blur it to 2% to give it a soft look!

Step 5: Finish up. I use a layer set to Multiply, and use the Airbrush tool with a muted pink to give the characters some blush! then I use the Syrup pen above the lineart layer to make small highlights on the eyes, and cheeks!
