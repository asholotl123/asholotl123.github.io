About Me!

Heres a little about me, the author of Wild Boy!

About Me:

My name is Asholotl! I LOVE making art and comics in my free time, as well as writing stories! I have 6 siblings, and I'm a member of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter Day Saints! I have a cute boyfriend who is currently serving a mission for our church, and he inspires so many wonderful stories and drawings I do! I am a huge Star Wars nerd, and have been for 12 years! I do however have Obsessive Compulsive Disorder, which really affects my comic making process, and makes me slower at creating. OCD is very stereotyped, and it gives me constant anxiety and fear every day, so please be patient with me as I sporadically create this!
