Welcome to Wild Boy!

This page is going to be a whole big explanation of my comic itself, the inspiration, and even a few tutorials on how to draw the characters, and how I draw my comics!

Summary:

Wild Boy is a comic about a young twenty seven year old man named Jared Kettle, encountering a little trespasser one day! That trespasser is a young four year old boy, who has a personality like a wolf! When a series of unfortunate events causes Jared to become without his family, he learns to heal as he takes care of the little wild boy.
