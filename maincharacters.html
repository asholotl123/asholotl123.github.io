About the Main Characters!

Heres a little bit about Jared and Leo, who are the main characters of this story!

Jared and Leo

Jared is 27 years old, and loves kids, jackets, and the color orange. He lost his wife and young son in a tragic accident a few months prior. As he was trying to heal, he met a three year old little boy, who he named Leo after his previous son. Leo is a curious, happy little guy who loves dogs, and snuggles!
